## ‚ùì FAQ

### Q: Why doesn't the bot respond immediately?

**A:** The bot checks mentions every 15-30 minutes (randomized). This prevents spam and keeps the bot within Twitter's rate limits. Wait 15-30 minutes after mentioning it.

### Q: Can I DM the bot?

**A:** No, the bot only responds to public mentions (@OverseerBot). Direct messages are not monitored.

### Q: How accurate are the price checks?

**A:** Prices come from Binance API (with CoinGecko fallback) and update every 5 minutes. They're accurate but may have a 0-5 minute delay.

### Q: How reliable are token safety checks?

**A:** Safety data comes from honeypot.is API. It's generally reliable but not 100% foolproof. **Always DYOR (Do Your Own Research)** before investing.

### Q: Can I request new features?

**A:** Yes! Mention suggestions to the bot or contact the bot owner. Popular requests may be added.

### Q: What tokens can the bot check prices for?

**A:** By default: SOL, BTC, ETH. The bot owner can add more tokens in the configuration.

### Q: Does the bot trade or invest?

**A:** **NO!** The bot only provides information and analysis. It never trades, holds funds, or gives financial advice.

### Q: Is my data safe when I mention the bot?

**A:** Yes! The bot only reads public tweets you @mention it in. It doesn't access DMs, followers, or private data.

### Q: Can the bot check tokens on all blockchains?

**A:** It supports EVM chains (Ethereum, BSC, Polygon, etc.) via honeypot.is API. Other chains may not be supported.

### Q: Why does the bot have a Fallout theme?

**A:** The bot promotes Atomic Fizz Caps, a Fallout-themed blockchain game. The personality makes it fun and engaging!
